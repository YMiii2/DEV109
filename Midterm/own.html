Introduction of K-POP Singer "Woodz"

Woodz, whose real name is Cho Seung-youn, is a multifaceted South Korean singer, rapper, producer, and songwriter known for his eclectic approach to music and charismatic stage presence. Born on August 5, 1996, Woodz has successfully navigated various musical genres, showcasing his versatility across pop, R&B, hip hop, and electronic music. His musical journey began in earnest when he debuted as a member of the boy group UNIQ in 2014, under the Chinese company Yuehua Entertainment. UNIQ's international appeal helped Woodz gain early exposure in both the Korean and Chinese music markets.
